## When to Start Joint Supplements

**Prevention is better than cure.** Here's our age guide:

- **Large breeds:** Start at 2-3 years old
- **Medium breeds:** Start at 4-5 years old  
- **Small breeds:** Start at 5-6 years old
- **Any dog with early symptoms:** Start now

If your dog already has diagnosed arthritis, supplements help but work best alongside:
- Weight management
- Appropriate exercise (swimming is excellent)
- Orthopaedic bedding
- Prescription medications if needed
